Our Story

Building Botswana's most trusted digital business community, one connection at a time.

How It All Began

Bizz Connect was born from a simple observation: small businesses and entrepreneurs in Botswana were struggling to reach customers affordably. Traditional advertising was often too expensive, and many online platforms required paid promotions that small businesses could not afford.

In 2026, Bizz Connect was founded by six entrepreneurs — Kelebogile Mazongo, Anita Ndwana, Boitumelo Tlhaselo, Wangu Kudziila, Natasha Rantlheng, and Atang Moalosi — who recognized this challenge and wanted to create a solution. As students and aspiring business innovators, they envisioned a platform where businesses of all sizes could connect with customers, distributors, and manufacturers without breaking the bank.

What started as a simple idea to support local entrepreneurs has grown into a vision for Botswana’s premier digital business ecosystem, aiming to connect thousands of users across the country.

Our Mission

To democratize business growth in Botswana by providing an affordable, accessible platform where every entrepreneur—from informal traders to registered companies—can showcase their products and services, connect with customers, and build lasting business relationships.

Our Vision

To be the heartbeat of Botswana's entrepreneurial ecosystem, creating a digitally connected business community that drives economic growth, fosters innovation, and empowers every Motswana entrepreneur to succeed in the digital age.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped our story

February 2026

The Beginning

BizzConnect was founded by Kelebogile Mazongo, Anita Ndwana, Boitumelo Tlhaselo, Wangu Kudziila, Natasha Rantlheng and Atang Moalosi after identifying the challenges small businesses face in reaching customers affordably.

February 2026

Concept Development

The team began designing the BizConnect platform concept, focusing on connecting service providers, customers, manufacturers, and distributors in one digital hub.

March 2026

MVP Website Development

Development of the first Minimum Viable Product (MVP) website began, featuring business listings, registration, and basic networking features.

March 2026

Platform Testing

The platform was tested with a small group of local entrepreneurs and students to gather feedback and improve usability.

March 2026

Community Forum Introduction

A community discussion forum was introduced to allow businesses, customers, manufacturers, and distributors to communicate and share opportunities.

March 2026

Early Business Adoption

The platform began attracting early adopters including small service providers such as barbers, tutors, mechanics, and clothing sellers.

Future Vision

Growth Across Botswana

BizzConnect aims to expand nationwide, connecting entrepreneurs, suppliers, and customers across Botswana and becoming a leading digital business ecosystem.
