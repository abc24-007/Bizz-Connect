Entrepreneurship Summit 2024

March 15-16, 2024
09:00 - 17:00
GICC, Gaborone
Free Entry

About This Event

The Botswana Entrepreneurship Summit is the premier gathering for entrepreneurs, investors, and business leaders in Botswana. This two-day event features keynote speeches from successful entrepreneurs, panel discussions on key business topics, networking opportunities, and a startup exhibition.

What You'll Learn

  • How to access funding for your business
  • Digital marketing strategies for SMEs
  • Export opportunities for Botswana businesses
  • Building a successful team
  • Financial management best practices
