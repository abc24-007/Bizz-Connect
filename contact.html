Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 2-4 hours during business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our support line directly.

Do you offer virtual meetings?

Yes! We offer virtual meetings via Zoom, Google Meet, or Microsoft Teams. Use the contact form to request a meeting, and we'll send you a calendar invitation.

Can I get support in my local language?

Yes! Our support team speaks multiple languages including English, Setswana, and more. Just let us know your preference when you contact us.

Do you have after-hours support?

Our team is available during posted hours, but you can always send us a message and we'll respond first thing next business day. We monitor emails 24/7 for urgent matters.
