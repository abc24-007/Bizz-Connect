Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 2-4 hours during business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our support line directly.

Do you offer support for business registration?

Yes! Our support team can guide you through the registration process and help you set up your business profile on the platform.

Can I schedule a meeting with your team?

Absolutely! Use the contact form to request a meeting, and we'll send you a calendar invitation with available time slots.

Do you have support in local languages?

Yes! Our support team speaks English and Setswana. Just let us know your preference when you contact us.
