Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner. Choose your account type (Customer, Service Provider, Distributor, or Manufacturer) and fill in the required information. You'll receive a verification email to activate your account.
Is it free to use Bizz Connect?
Yes! Basic accounts are free. We offer premium features for businesses that want to grow faster. Check our pricing page for more details.
How do I book a service?
Browse service providers, select the one you like, and click "Book Now". Choose your preferred date and time, then confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation once the provider accepts.
How do I become a service provider?
Create an account and select "Service Provider" as your account type. Complete your business profile, add your services, and verify your business. Once verified, you can start receiving bookings.
How do payments work?
Payments are processed securely through our platform. Customers can pay via credit card, mobile money, or bank transfer. Funds are held securely and released to providers after service completion.
